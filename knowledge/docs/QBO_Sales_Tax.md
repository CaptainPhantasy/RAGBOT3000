# QuickBooks Sales Tax Setup and Management

## Visual UI Anchors
- **Sales tax** (left navigation)
- **Gear icon** → **Account and Settings** → **Sales tax**
- **Sales tax rates**: Add/Edit tax agencies
- **Filing history**: Past tax returns
- **Pay tax button**: Submit tax payments

## Step-by-Step Workflow (2025)

### Initial Setup
1. Click **Sales tax** in left navigation
2. Click **Set up sales tax**
3. Select your **Province or territory**
4. Click **Save**
5. For provinces with multiple taxes (PST/HST):
   - Click **Sales tax** again
   - Add additional tax agencies
   - Enter tax rates
   - Save changes

### Ongoing Management
1. Sales tax auto-calculates on transactions
2. Review tax liability in Sales tax center
3. File taxes through QuickBooks or manually
4. Record payments

## Why This Matters (Accounting Context)
Sales tax setup ensures:
- Correct tax collection from customers
- Accurate tax liability tracking
- Easier tax filing
- Compliance with tax regulations

## Common Error States / Pitfalls

### Wrong tax agency selected
- **What you see:** Incorrect tax rate applied
- **Diagnosis:** Province or tax agency setup error
- **Fix:**
  1. Go to Sales tax setup
  2. Select correct agency
  3. Verify rates

### Tax not calculated on sales
- **What you see:** Tax showing as $0.00
- **Diagnosis:** Customer or item marked as exempt
- **Fix:**
  1. Check customer tax settings
  2. Verify item tax status
  3. Update as needed

### Mixing taxable and non-taxable on same line
- **What you see:** Incorrect tax calculations
- **Diagnosis:** Multiple tax items on single line
- **Fix:**
  1. Use separate lines for taxable/non-taxable
  2. Create separate items if needed
