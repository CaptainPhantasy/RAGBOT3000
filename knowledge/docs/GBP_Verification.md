**Verification Authority**: Google automatically determines available verification methods based on business type, region, and public info. You cannot manually select a method if it is not offered.

**Common Methods**:
- **Video Recording**: Recommended for most businesses. Requires recording equipment, location, and proof of management.
- **Phone/Text**: Code sent to the business line (IVR systems not supported).
- **Email**: Code sent to the business email address.
- **Live Video Call**: Real-time call with a support representative during business hours.
- **Postcard (Mail)**: Physical code mailed to the business address (14-day arrival).

**Technical Specs**:
- **Code Expiry**: Verification codes expire after **30 days**.
- **Video Duration**: Up to 30 seconds (though some guides suggest < 2 mins for upload issues).
- **Video Resolution**: 720p or higher.
- **Video Size**: Up to 75 MB.

# ðŸ› ï¸ WORKFLOW

### Phase 1: Initiate Verification
1. Sign in to your Google Business Profile (GBP) account.
2. Select "Get verified".
3. Choose from the available methods presented by Google.

### Phase 2: Execution by Method

**Option A: Video Recording (Preferred)**
1. **Plan**: Ensure you have access to the business location and keys/equipment.
2. **Record**: Start recording from the GBP app.
   - Show **Location**: Street sign, storefront, nearby businesses.
   - Show **Equipment**: Branded vehicle, marketing materials, tools.
   - Show **Management**: Unlocking the door, employee-only areas, cash register, software.
3. **Upload**: Submit the video immediately via the app.

**Option B: Postcard (Mail)**
1. Select "Postcard".
2. Confirm the exact mailing address.
3. Request the code.
4. **WAIT**: Do not edit your business name, address, or category while waiting.
5. Enter the 5-digit code upon arrival.

**Option C: Live Video Call**
1. Select "Live video call".
2. Ensure you are at the business location during support hours.
3. Join the call when prompted and follow the agent's instructions to show proof of ownership via your camera.

# ðŸ›‘ TRAP-DOORS (Instant Failure Risks)

> **CRITICAL WARNING**: "Do not edit your business name, address, or category on your Business Profile [while waiting for a postcard]. If you do, the code we sent you won't work."

- **IVR Systems**: Phone verification fails if the line has an interactive voice response system; it must be a direct line.
- **Code Expiry**: Codes are dead after 30 days.
- **Re-Verification Trigger**: Changing core business details (Address, Name) *after* verification can trigger a re-verification requirement.
- **Video Upload Failures**: Videos over 2 minutes or 75MB often fail at "99% upload". Keep it short.

# ðŸ”§ TRIAGE MATRIX

| Error / Issue | Diagnosis | Fix |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **"Get verified" button reappears** | Verification incomplete | Google couldn't verify. Restart the process with a different method if available. |
| **"Review issues" notification** | Video rejected | Select "Review issues" and follow on-screen instructions. Record a new video focusing on clearer proof of management (keys, software). |
| **Code didn't arrive (14+ days)** | Lost postcard | Request a new code *only if* the timeframe has passed. Do not request multiple codes rapidly. |
| **Video upload stuck at 99%** | File size/Length | Reduce video length to < 2 mins, resolution to 720p, and try uploading via Chrome on a stable WiFi connection. |
| **No methods available** | System restriction | Contact Google Support directly. |
