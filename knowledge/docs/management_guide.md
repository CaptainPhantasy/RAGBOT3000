## Getting Started as a Manager

### The Management Role in ServiceTitan

Managers use ServiceTitan to:

- **Monitor Performance:** Real-time KPIs and metrics
- **Analyze Trends:** Historical data and patterns
- **Make Decisions:** Data-driven strategic choices
- **Lead Teams:** Track and improve team performance
- **Optimize Operations:** Identify and address inefficiencies
- **Drive Growth:** Find revenue opportunities and expansion paths

### Core Management Functions

**Strategic Oversight:**
- Monitor business health via dashboards
- Review financial performance
- Track operational efficiency
- Analyze market trends

**Team Leadership:**
- Review technician and office staff performance
- Provide coaching and feedback
- Set goals and incentives
- Recognize and reward excellence

**Operational Management:**
- Ensure service quality standards
- Optimize scheduling and capacity
- Manage resource allocation
- Improve customer satisfaction

**Financial Management:**
- Monitor revenue and profitability
- Control costs and expenses
- Manage cash flow
- Optimize pricing and margins

## Dashboards & KPIs

### Accessing Dashboards

**Navigation:**
1. Log into ServiceTitan
2. Click **"Dashboards"** in left sidebar
3. Select dashboard type:
   - **Overview Dashboard:** High-level business metrics
   - **Technician Dashboard:** Field performance
   - **Sales Dashboard:** Revenue and conversion metrics
   - **Marketing Dashboard:** Lead generation and ROI
   - **Custom Dashboards:** Personalized views

### Overview Dashboard

**Key Metrics Displayed:**

**Revenue Metrics:**
- **Total Revenue:** Day/week/month/year
- **Average Ticket:** Mean invoice value
- **Jobs Completed:** Count of finished jobs
- **Revenue by Business Unit:** HVAC, Plumbing, etc.

**Operational Metrics:**
- **Jobs Booked:** New appointments scheduled
- **Jobs in Progress:** Active work
- **Technician Utilization:** Capacity usage %
- **Completion Rate:** Jobs finished on time

**Customer Metrics:**
- **New Customers:** First-time clients
- **Repeat Customers:** Return clients
- **Customer Satisfaction:** Ratings and reviews
- **Membership Sales:** Recurring agreements

**Financial Health:**
- **Outstanding AR:** Unpaid invoices
- **Cash Collected:** Payments received
- **Profitability:** Margins and net income
- **Expenses:** Operating costs

### Technician Scorecard

**Individual Performance Tracking:**

**For Each Technician:**
- **Revenue Generated:** Total sales
- **Jobs Completed:** Count of finished jobs
- **Average Ticket:** Mean sale value
- **Conversion Rate:** Estimates sold / presented
- **Hours Worked:** Time tracked
- **Revenue per Hour:** Productivity metric
- **Customer Ratings:** Satisfaction scores

**Use Cases:**
- **Daily Review:** Monitor performance
- **Weekly Meetings:** Coaching conversations
- **Monthly Evaluation:** Performance reviews
- **Incentive Calculation:** Commission and bonuses

### Marketing Scorecard

**Campaign Performance:**

**Metrics:**
- **Leads Generated:** By campaign/source
- **Cost per Lead:** Marketing spend / leads
- **Conversion Rate:** Leads to jobs %
- **Revenue per Lead:** Average value
- **ROI:** Return on marketing investment

**By Campaign:**
- Google Ads
- Facebook/Social Media
- Direct Mail
- Referrals
- Organic Search

### Custom Dashboards

**Creating Custom Dashboard:**

1. **Dashboards > Create New Dashboard**
2. **Add Widgets:**
   - Revenue charts
   - KPI cards
   - Performance tables
   - Trend graphs
3. **Configure Each Widget:**
   - Select data source
   - Set filters (date range, business unit)
   - Choose visualization type
4. **Arrange Layout:**
   - Drag and drop widgets
   - Resize for emphasis
   - Organize logically
5. **Save and Share:**
   - Name dashboard
   - Set as default (optional)
   - Share with team

**Dashboard Best Practices:**
- Keep it simple: Focus on most important metrics
- Use visuals: Charts and graphs over tables
- Update regularly: Review and refine
- Role-specific: Different dashboards for different roles

## Reports & Analytics

### Accessing Reports

**Navigation:**
1. Click **"Reports"** in left sidebar
2. Browse categories:
   - **Built-In Reports:** Pre-configured by ServiceTitan
   - **Custom Reports:** Created by your team
   - **Scheduled Reports:** Automated delivery

### Built-In Reports

**Common Reports by Category:**

**Financial Reports:**
- **Revenue Summary:** Total revenue by period
- **Invoice Report:** All invoices with details
- **Payment Report:** Payments collected
- **Outstanding AR:** Unpaid invoices
- **Profitability Report:** Revenue vs. costs

**Operational Reports:**
- **Job Summary:** All jobs with statuses
- **Technician Performance:** Individual metrics
- **Dispatch Board Activity:** Scheduling efficiency
- **Completion Rate:** On-time performance
- **Service Agreement Report:** Membership tracking

**Sales Reports:**
- **Estimates Report:** All quotes with statuses
- **Sold Estimates:** Converted quotes
- **Conversion Rate:** Sales performance
- **Average Ticket:** Mean sale value
- **Sales by Service:** Popular offerings

**Customer Reports:**
- **Customer List:** All customers with details
- **New Customer Report:** Recent additions
- **Customer Retention:** Repeat business
- **Customer Lifetime Value:** Total per customer
- **Satisfaction Report:** Ratings and reviews

**Marketing Reports:**
- **Lead Source Report:** Where customers come from
- **Campaign Performance:** Marketing ROI
- **Cost per Acquisition:** Marketing efficiency
- **Booking Rate:** Leads to appointments

### Running Reports

**Step-by-Step:**

1. **Select Report**
   - Browse or search report library
   - Click report name

2. **Set Parameters**
   - **Date Range:** Today, this week, this month, custom
   - **Business Unit:** HVAC, Plumbing, All
   - **Technician:** Specific or all
   - **Job Type:** Service, installation, maintenance
   - **Other Filters:** Status, customer type, etc.

3. **Run Report**
   - Click **"Run"** or **"Generate"**
   - Wait for processing (seconds to minutes depending on data)

4. **Review Results**
   - View in browser
   - Sort and filter within results
   - Drill down into details

5. **Export (optional)**
   - **PDF:** Formatted for printing/sharing
   - **Excel:** For further analysis
   - **CSV:** Raw data export

6. **Schedule (optional)**
   - **Daily/Weekly/Monthly:** Automated delivery
   - **Email Recipients:** Who receives
   - **Format:** PDF or Excel

### Custom Reports

**Creating Custom Reports:**

**Note:** Advanced feature requiring training or professional services. Allows querying ServiceTitan database directly.

**Process:**
1. **Reports > Custom Reports > Create New**
2. **Select Data Tables:** Jobs, invoices, customers, etc.
3. **Choose Fields:** Columns to include
4. **Add Filters:** Narrow results
5. **Configure Calculations:** Sums, averages, counts
6. **Test and Refine:** Run sample, adjust as needed
7. **Save and Share:** Name report, set permissions

**Use Cases:**
- Unique business metrics
- Custom KPIs not in built-in reports
- Complex data analysis
- Compliance and auditing

### Report Best Practices

**Regular Review Schedule:**
- **Daily:** Revenue, jobs completed, technician activity
- **Weekly:** Performance metrics, sales conversion, customer satisfaction
- **Monthly:** Financial statements, profitability, trends
- **Quarterly:** Strategic review, goal progress, forecasting

**Data-Driven Decisions:**
- Don't just collect data - act on insights
- Identify patterns and trends
- Compare to benchmarks and goals
- Experiment and measure results

**Share Transparently:**
- Provide reports to relevant teams
- Discuss findings in meetings
- Celebrate successes
- Address challenges openly

## Performance Management

### Technician Performance

**Key Metrics:**
- **Revenue per Technician:** Total sales
- **Average Ticket:** Mean job value
- **Jobs per Day:** Productivity
- **Conversion Rate:** Estimates sold
- **Customer Satisfaction:** Ratings
- **Hours Utilization:** Billable vs. total hours

**Coaching Opportunities:**
- **Low Conversion:** Sales training
- **Low Average Ticket:** Upselling techniques
- **Low Satisfaction:** Customer service skills
- **Low Productivity:** Efficiency and time management

**Recognition & Rewards:**
- **Leaderboards:** Public recognition
- **Bonuses:** Financial incentives
- **Commissions:** Percentage of sales
- **Awards:** Top performer recognition
- **Career Development:** Promotions and growth

### Office Staff Performance

**Key Metrics:**
- **Call Booking Rate:** Calls to appointments %
- **Customer Satisfaction:** Interaction quality
- **Response Time:** Speed of service
- **Error Rate:** Accuracy of data entry
- **Task Completion:** Assigned tasks finished

**Coaching Areas:**
- **Low Booking Rate:** Sales and objection handling
- **Slow Response:** Time management and prioritization
- **High Errors:** Attention to detail, training needs
- **Customer Complaints:** Communication and problem-solving

### Setting Goals

**SMART Goals:**
- **Specific:** Clear and detailed
- **Measurable:** Quantifiable metrics
- **Achievable:** Realistic based on history
- **Relevant:** Aligned with business objectives
- **Time-Bound:** Defined timeframe

**Examples:**
- "Increase average ticket by 15% in Q2"
- "Achieve 50% estimate conversion rate by June"
- "Book 20 new customers per week"
- "Maintain 4.8+ star customer rating"

**Tracking Progress:**
- Review metrics weekly
- Provide feedback and coaching
- Adjust strategies as needed
- Celebrate milestones achieved

## Business Intelligence

### Trend Analysis

**Identifying Patterns:**

**Seasonal Trends:**
- HVAC demand peaks in summer and winter
- Plumbing issues increase in winter (frozen pipes)
- Adjust staffing and inventory accordingly

**Day-of-Week Patterns:**
- Monday call volume high (weekend issues)
- Friday bookings lower (weekend plans)
- Optimize scheduling and promotions

**Revenue Trends:**
- Month-over-month growth or decline
- Year-over-year comparisons
- Business unit performance changes

**Customer Behavior:**
- Repeat business frequency
- Average time between services
- Service upgrade patterns

### Benchmarking

**Compare Performance:**

**Internal Benchmarks:**
- Current period vs. previous period
- This year vs. last year
- One business unit vs. another
- One technician vs. team average

**External Benchmarks:**
- Industry standards and averages
- Competitor performance (if available)
- ServiceTitan user averages (if provided)

**Key Benchmarks:**
- **Revenue per Technician:** $500-$800/day typical
- **Average Ticket:** $300-$500 service, $3,000+ install
- **Conversion Rate:** 40-60% good, 60%+ excellent
- **Customer Acquisition Cost:** Varies by market
- **Customer Lifetime Value:** Track and maximize

### Forecasting

**Predicting Future Performance:**

**Revenue Forecasting:**
- Based on historical trends
- Account for seasonality
- Consider market conditions
- Adjust for growth initiatives

**Capacity Planning:**
- Projected job volume
- Technician availability
- Hiring needs
- Equipment and inventory requirements

**Cash Flow Projections:**
- Expected revenue
- Anticipated expenses
- Payment timing (AR collection)
- Investment needs

**Use Forecasts For:**
- Budgeting and financial planning
- Staffing decisions
- Marketing investment
- Strategic initiatives

## Team Management

### User Management

**Adding Users:**

1. **Settings > Users > Add User**
2. **Enter Details:**
   - Name, email, phone
   - Username and temporary password
3. **Assign Role:**
   - Technician, Dispatcher, CSR, Manager, Admin
4. **Set Permissions:**
   - Module access (Dispatch, Reports, etc.)
   - Edit vs. view-only
   - Financial data visibility
5. **Assign to Business Unit/Team**
6. **Save and Activate**

**Managing Permissions:**
- **Administrator:** Full access, all modules
- **Manager:** Broad access, limited system config
- **Dispatcher:** Scheduling and job management
- **CSR:** Customer and call booking focus
- **Technician:** Mobile app, assigned jobs
- **Accounting:** Financial modules
- **Custom Roles:** Tailored permissions

### Team Organization

**Business Units:**

**Territories:**
- Geographic zones
- Assign technicians to territories
- Optimize routing and coverage

**Shifts:**

- Coordinate coverage

### Communication

**Performance Feedback:**

## Troubleshooting

### Common Management Challenges

**Data Discrepancies:**

**Symptoms:** Reports don't match expectations or other sources

**Solutions:**
- Verify date ranges and filters
- Check for timing differences (cash vs. accrual)
- Ensure all jobs/invoices finalized
- Compare to accounting software (if integrated)
- Contact support if persistent

**Low Performance Metrics:**

**Symptoms:** Team not hitting goals

**Solutions:**
- Identify root causes (skills, process, motivation)
- Provide targeted training
- Adjust goals if unrealistic
- Remove obstacles and barriers
- Recognize and reward improvement

**Report Not Showing Data:**

**Symptoms:** Report runs but shows no results

**Solutions:**
- Check filters (too restrictive?)
- Verify date range (correct period?)
- Ensure data exists for criteria
- Check permissions (can you see this data?)
- Try broader filters and narrow down

**Can't Access Feature:**

**Symptoms:** Menu item missing or action unavailable

**Solutions:**
- Verify user permissions
- Check subscription level (feature may not be included)
- Confirm module is enabled for company
- Contact admin to adjust permissions
- Contact support if feature should be available
