## Getting Started as a Field Technician

### Your Role in ServiceTitan

As a field technician, you are the face of the company to customers. ServiceTitan Mobile empowers you to:

- **Access Your Schedule:** View daily appointments and job details
- **Navigate to Jobs:** Get directions and arrival information
- **Complete Work:** Document services performed
- **Sell Estimates:** Present options and close deals on-site
- **Collect Payments:** Process payments immediately
- **Track Everything:** Time, materials, equipment, and customer communications
- **Stay Connected:** Real-time sync with office staff

### What You Need

**Hardware:**
- Company-issued tablet (iPad or Android) or smartphone
- Reliable cellular data connection or Wi-Fi
- Charger and protective case

**Software:**
- **Field Mobile App** (recommended - new 2025+ version)
- OR **ServiceTitan Mobile** (legacy version)

**Accounts:**
- ServiceTitan login credentials
- Access to your technician profile
- Permission to complete jobs and collect payments

**Training:**
- Mobile app basics
- Job completion procedures
- Estimate building
- Payment processing
- Company-specific policies

## Mobile App Setup

### Field Mobile App (New - Recommended)

#### Initial Setup

1. **Download the App**
   - **iOS:** Search "ServiceTitan Field Mobile" in App Store
   - **Android:** Search "ServiceTitan Field Mobile" in Google Play
   - Download and install

2. **Log In**
   - Open the app
   - Enter your ServiceTitan credentials (provided by admin)
   - Allow permissions for:
     - Location (for GPS tracking)
     - Camera (for photos and scanning)
     - Notifications (for job updates)
     - Phone (for calling customers)

3. **Configure Settings**
   - Set notification preferences
   - Configure default views
   - Set up payment methods (if applicable)
   - Review profile information

4. **Sync Data**
   - Allow initial data sync (may take a few minutes)
   - Verify your schedule appears
   - Check pricebook is available

#### App Features & Navigation

**Home Screen:**
- Daily schedule with upcoming jobs
- Quick actions (call office, view inventory, etc.)
- Notifications and alerts
- Current job status

**Bottom Navigation Bar:**
- **Schedule:** View daily appointments
- **Jobs:** Active and recent jobs
- **Estimates:** Draft and presented quotes
- **More:** Additional tools and settings

**Key Features:**
- **Faster Performance:** Rebuilt from ground up for speed
- **Offline Mode:** Work without internet, syncs when connected
- **Intuitive Design:** Streamlined for common workflows
- **Auto-Sync:** Automatic background synchronization
- **Dark Mode:** Easier viewing in various lighting

### ServiceTitan Mobile (Legacy)

**Note:** This is the older mobile app being phased out. If you're using Field Mobile App, skip this section.

#### Setup Steps
1. Download "ServiceTitan Mobile" from app store
2. Log in with credentials

## Daily Workflow Overview

### Typical Day for a Field Technician

#### Morning Routine (Before First Job)

1. **Clock In**
   - Open mobile app
   - Tap "Clock In" button
   - Verify location is correct
   - Review daily schedule

2. **Review Schedule**
   - View all appointments for the day
   - Check job details and customer notes
   - Identify any special requirements
   - Plan route and travel time

3. **Prepare**
   - Load necessary tools and materials
   - Review any open estimates or follow-ups
   - Check for customer messages or updates
   - Ensure tablet is charged

#### During the Day (Per Job)

1. **En Route to Job**
   - Mark "On My Way" in app
   - Use GPS navigation
   - Call customer to confirm arrival time
   - Review job details and history

2. **Arrive On-Site**
   - Mark "Arrived" in app (starts job time tracking)
   - Introduce yourself to customer
   - Assess the situation
   - Document initial conditions with photos

3. **Perform Work**
   - Complete necessary services
   - Document work performed
   - Track materials used
   - Take photos of work completed
   - Build estimate if additional work needed

4. **Present Estimate (if applicable)**
   - Show customer options using mobile app
   - Explain benefits of each option
   - Answer questions
   - Get customer approval/signature

5. **Complete Job**
   - Finalize invoice
   - Review all charges with customer
   - Collect payment
   - Get customer signature
   - Mark job complete

6. **Move to Next Job**
   - Review next appointment
   - Navigate to location
   - Repeat process

#### End of Day

1. **Clock Out**
   - Complete final job
   - Tap "Clock Out"
   - Verify all jobs are closed
   - Submit timesheet if required

2. **Review Day**
   - Check for missed tasks
   - Respond to any messages
   - Plan for tomorrow
   - Charge tablet overnight

## Job Management

### Viewing Your Schedule

#### Schedule Screen

**Information Displayed:**
- **Job Time:** Scheduled start time
- **Customer Name:** Who you're visiting
- **Location:** Service address
- **Job Type:** Service call, installation, maintenance, etc.
- **Job Status:** Booked, dispatched, in progress, etc.
- **Special Notes:** Important customer or job information

**Schedule Actions:**
- **Tap Job:** View full job details
- **Call Customer:** Quick dial customer phone
- **Get Directions:** Launch navigation app
- **Filter:** View specific job types or time ranges

### Preparing for a Job

#### Before You Arrive

1. **Review Job Details**
   - Customer name and contact info
   - Service address and location notes
   - Job type and requested services
   - Customer service history
   - Previous estimates or invoices
   - Equipment on-site
   - Special instructions

2. **Check Customer History**
   - Previous jobs and services
   - Installed equipment
   - Membership status
   - Communication preferences
   - Payment history

3. **Verify Materials/Tools**
   - Required parts or equipment
   - Special tools needed
   - Access to inventory

4. **Communicate**
   - Call ahead to confirm appointment
   - Update arrival time if running late
   - Notify office of any issues

### Managing Jobs

#### Job Statuses

**On My Way:**
- Tap when leaving previous job or shop
- Customer receives notification (if configured)
- GPS tracking begins
- Updates dispatch board

**Arrived:**
- Tap upon arrival at customer location
- Starts job timer
- Updates office in real-time
- Begins time tracking for payroll/billing

**In Progress:**
- Automatically set when you arrive
- Indicates active work being performed
- Can add notes, photos, forms during this time

**Complete:**
- Tap when all work is finished
- Required before creating invoice
- Stops job timer
- Triggers completion workflows

### Job Details & Information

#### Accessing Job Information

From job screen, view:
- **Customer Tab:** Contact info, location, notes
- **Services Tab:** Requested and performed services
- **Equipment Tab:** Installed equipment on-site
- **History Tab:** Past jobs and service records
- **Forms Tab:** Required paperwork and forms
- **Media Tab:** Photos and documents
- **Estimates Tab:** Related quotes
- **Invoice Tab:** Billing information

### Using FieldAssist / Atlas

**Note:** FieldAssist (called "Atlas" in newer versions) is an AI-powered assistant available with certain ServiceTitan plans.

**Features:**
- **Smart Recommendations:** Suggests services based on job type
- **Equipment Insights:** Provides equipment information and recommendations
- **Sales Prompts:** Guides estimate building with best practices
- **Diagnostic Help:** Troubleshooting assistance

**How to Use:**
1. Open job in mobile app
2. Tap Atlas/FieldAssist icon
3. View recommendations
4. Add suggested items to estimate
5. Follow prompts for best results

## Estimates & Sales

### Building Estimates

#### When to Create an Estimate

- **Additional Work Discovered:** Found issues during service call
- **Presenting Options:** Customer wants quote before approving work
- **Large Projects:** Installation or major repairs
- **Company Policy:** Required for work over certain dollar amount

#### Creating an Estimate in Mobile App

**Step-by-Step:**

1. **Open Job**
   - From your schedule, tap the job
   - Or create new estimate from "Estimates" tab

2. **Start New Estimate**
   - Tap "Create Estimate" button
   - Select estimate type (standard, options, packages)

3. **Add Services**
   - Search pricebook for services
   - Tap items to add
   - Adjust quantities
   - Add custom descriptions

4. **Add Materials**
   - Search for materials/equipment
   - Specify quantities
   - Update pricing if permitted

5. **Add Labor**
   - Select labor type
   - Specify hours
   - Review labor rates

6. **Create Options (if applicable)**
   - **Good-Better-Best:** Present multiple tiers
   - **Option 1, 2, 3:** Different approaches
   - Include recommended option

7. **Add Discounts (if authorized)**
   - Apply promotional discounts
   - Add membership savings
   - Include manufacturer rebates

8. **Review Total**
   - Verify all items included
   - Check pricing accuracy
   - Ensure customer can see details

9. **Add Notes & Media**
   - Take photos of issue
   - Add explanatory notes
   - Include diagrams if helpful

### Presenting Estimates

#### Best Practices for Selling

1. **Set the Stage**
   - Find comfortable place to talk
   - Ensure customer is ready to discuss
   - Have tablet ready with estimate displayed

2. **Explain the Situation**
   - Describe what you found
   - Show photos/evidence
   - Explain why it needs attention
   - Discuss potential consequences of not fixing

3. **Present Options**
   - Show all options clearly
   - Explain differences between each
   - Recommend the best solution
   - Highlight value, not just price

4. **Use Visual Aids**
   - Show photos on tablet
   - Use diagrams or manufacturer info
   - Display equipment/materials if available

5. **Address Concerns**
   - Listen to customer questions
   - Answer honestly and clearly
   - Overcome objections professionally
   - Provide testimonials or reviews

6. **Ask for the Sale**
   - "Which option works best for you?"
   - Get decision or next steps
   - Offer financing if available
   - Provide flexible scheduling

7. **Get Approval**
   - Tap "Present" in app
   - Customer reviews on tablet
   - Customer signs with finger/stylus
   - Save approved estimate

#### Handling Estimate Outcomes

**Sold:**
- Customer approves and signs
- Estimate converts to job
- Schedule work (now or later)
- Begin work if immediate approval

**Follow-Up:**
- Customer needs time to decide
- Mark for follow-up
- Office will contact customer
- May call yourself to follow up

**Dismissed:**
- Customer declines
- Mark reason for dismissal
- Understand objections for future learning
- Thank customer for their time

### Sales Tips for Technicians

**Do's:**
- ✅ Build trust through professionalism
- ✅ Educate customer on options
- ✅ Focus on value and benefits
- ✅ Show genuine care for their needs
- ✅ Use photos to illustrate problems
- ✅ Offer financing when appropriate
- ✅ Be confident in your recommendations

**Don'ts:**
- ❌ High-pressure tactics
- ❌ Overwhelming with too many options
- ❌ Dismissing customer concerns
- ❌ Badmouthing competitors
- ❌ Making unrealistic promises
- ❌ Skipping explanation of work needed
- ❌ Being defensive about pricing

## Invoicing & Payments

### Creating Invoices

#### When to Invoice

- **Job Complete:** All work finished and customer satisfied
- **Estimate Approved:** If estimate was sold, convert to invoice
- **Per Company Policy:** Follow your company's invoicing procedures

#### Creating Invoice in Mobile App

**Step-by-Step:**

1. **Complete Job**
   - Ensure all work is documented
   - All services added to job
   - All materials tracked
   - Photos uploaded

2. **Open Job Invoice Tab**
   - From job screen, tap "Invoice"
   - Review items automatically added

3. **Verify Invoice Items**
   - Services performed
   - Materials used
   - Equipment installed
   - Labor charges
   - Taxes applied

4. **Add Missing Items**
   - Search pricebook
   - Add any items not auto-added
   - Include all billable work

5. **Apply Discounts (if applicable)**
   - Membership discounts
   - Promotional offers
   - Manager-approved adjustments

6. **Review Total**
   - Subtotal correct
   - Taxes calculated properly
   - Total amount due accurate

7. **Present to Customer**
   - Review invoice with customer
   - Explain all charges
   - Answer any questions

8. **Get Customer Signature**
   - Customer signs on tablet
   - Acknowledges work performed
   - Agrees to charges

### Collecting Payments

#### Payment Methods

**Credit/Debit Card:**
1. Tap "Collect Payment"
2. Enter amount (usually full invoice total)
3. Select "Credit/Debit Card"
4. Customer enters card information OR use card reader
5. Process payment
6. Get approval
7. Customer receives digital receipt

**Check:**
1. Tap "Collect Payment"
2. Enter amount
3. Select "Check"
4. Enter check number
5. Take photo of check (if required)
6. Complete transaction

**Cash:**
1. Tap "Collect Payment"
2. Enter amount
3. Select "Cash"
4. Confirm receipt of cash
5. Provide receipt to customer

**ACH/Bank Transfer:**
1. Tap "Collect Payment"
2. Select "ACH"
3. Customer enters bank account info (or use saved)
4. Authorize transfer
5. Confirm transaction

**Financing:**
1. Tap "Offer Financing"
2. Customer completes financing application
3. Get approval (may be instant or require waiting)
4. Complete financing paperwork
5. Payment processed through financing company

#### Partial Payments

If customer can't pay full amount:
1. Discuss payment plan with customer
2. Collect partial payment
3. Note remaining balance
4. Office will follow up for remaining amount

#### Payment Troubleshooting

**Card Declined:**
- Ask customer for alternative payment method
- Verify card information entered correctly
- Check card reader connection
- Contact office if system issue

**Check Concerns:**
- Verify check is filled out correctly
- Confirm sufficient funds (if policy requires)
- Take clear photo of check
- Get customer contact info for follow-up

**System Issues:**
- Document payment received
- Note payment type and amount
- Contact office immediately
- Process payment when system restored

### Receipts & Documentation

**Digital Receipts:**
- Automatically emailed to customer
- Includes invoice detail
- Payment confirmation
- Company contact information

**Paper Receipts (if needed):**
- Print from mobile device (if equipped)
- Or provide invoice number for office follow-up

#### Installed Equipment

When installing equipment:

1. **Add to Job**
   - From job screen, go to "Equipment" tab
   - Tap "Add Equipment"
   - Search for equipment type
   - Select manufacturer and model

2. **Record Details**
   - Serial number
   - Model number
   - Installation date
   - Warranty information
   - Location at property

3. **Take Photos**
   - Equipment installed
   - Serial number plate
   - Installation details

4. **Complete Installation Form**
   - Manufacturer registration (if applicable)
   - Warranty activation
   - Customer instructions

**Benefits:**
- Customer has record of installed equipment
- Service history tracked per equipment
- Maintenance reminders can be scheduled
- Warranty tracking

#### Creating Purchase Order

If you need materials not on truck:

1. **Open Job**
2. **Tap "Create Purchase Order"**
3. **Select Vendor**
4. **Add Items Needed**
5. **Submit for Approval**
6. **Pick Up Materials** (once approved)

## Troubleshooting

### Common Mobile App Issues

#### App Won't Sync

**Symptoms:** Data not updating, changes not saving
**Solutions:**
1. Check internet connection (cellular or Wi-Fi)
2. Force close app and reopen
3. Log out and log back in
4. Check for app updates in app store
5. Contact office if persistent

#### Can't Log In

**Symptoms:** Login fails, wrong password error
**Solutions:**
1. Verify credentials (username and password)
2. Check for email updates from admin about password changes
3. Use "Forgot Password" to reset
4. Contact office/admin for account help

#### Schedule Not Showing

**Symptoms:** No jobs appearing on schedule
**Solutions:**
1. Pull down to refresh schedule
2. Check date filter (make sure on correct day)
3. Verify internet connection
4. Force close and reopen app
5. Contact dispatch to verify jobs assigned to you

#### Payment Processing Fails

**Symptoms:** Card declined, payment won't process
**Solutions:**
1. Verify card information entered correctly
2. Check card reader connection (if using)
3. Try different payment method
4. Contact office for payment system status
5. Document payment received, process later if system issue

#### Photos Won't Upload

**Symptoms:** Photos stuck uploading, not saving
**Solutions:**
1. Check internet connection strength
2. Wait for better connection (photos will upload when connected)
3. Reduce photo size/quality in camera settings
4. Free up storage space on device
5. Force close app and retry

### Getting Help

**In the Field:**
1. **Check help icon in app** - Context-specific articles
2. **Call office/dispatch** - For immediate assistance
3. **Contact tech support** - Through ServiceTitan portal

**For Training:**
1. **ServiceTitan Academy** - Online courses
2. **Manager/Supervisor** - Coaching and guidance
3. **Experienced Technician** - Peer learning
4. **Help Center** - help.servicetitan.com

### Common Questions

**Q: What if I'm running late?**
A: Call customer and office/dispatch as soon as possible. Update ETA in app if available.

**Q: What if customer isn't home?**
A: Contact dispatch immediately. Mark "unable to complete" with note. Reschedule.

**Q: What if I don't have materials needed?**
A: Build estimate for materials needed. Create purchase order if approved. Return to complete.

**Q: What if customer disputes charges?**
A: Explain charges clearly. If unresolved, contact manager. Don't waive charges without authorization.

**Q: What if I find additional work needed?**
A: Build estimate immediately. Present to customer. Get approval before performing additional work.

**Q: What if app isn't working?**
A: Document work on paper. Contact office. Enter information when system restored.
