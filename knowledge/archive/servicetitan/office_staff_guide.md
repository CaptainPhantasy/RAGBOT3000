## Getting Started as Office Staff

### The Office Staff Role in ServiceTitan

Office staff are the backbone of daily operations, using ServiceTitan to:

- **Answer Calls:** Provide excellent customer service
- **Book Appointments:** Schedule jobs with customers
- **Manage Customer Records:** Maintain accurate information
- **Process Invoices:** Create and send bills
- **Collect Payments:** Accept and record payments
- **Coordinate Teams:** Connect customers, dispatch, and technicians
- **Handle Administration:** Data entry, filing, follow-ups

### Core Responsibilities

**Customer-Facing:**
- Answer incoming calls promptly
- Book service appointments
- Respond to customer inquiries
- Resolve complaints and issues
- Follow up on estimates and invoices
- Maintain professional communication

**Administrative:**
- Enter and update customer data
- Create and manage invoices
- Process payments
- Schedule and reschedule appointments
- Handle paperwork and documentation
- Coordinate with dispatch and technicians

**Financial:**
- Collect payments from customers
- Process credit cards, checks, cash
- Track outstanding invoices
- Send payment reminders
- Generate financial reports
- Reconcile transactions

## Call Booking & Customer Service

### Answering Incoming Calls

#### Call Flow Best Practices

**Greeting:**
"Thank you for calling [Company Name], this is [Your Name]. How can I help you today?"

**Listening:**
- Let customer explain their need
- Take notes in ServiceTitan
- Ask clarifying questions
- Show empathy and understanding

**Information Gathering:**
- **Customer details:** Name, phone, email, address
- **Problem description:** What's wrong? When did it start?
- **Urgency:** Is it an emergency? How soon needed?
- **Preferences:** Preferred date/time, technician

**Setting Expectations:**
- Explain your process
- Provide time estimates
- Discuss pricing (if applicable)
- Confirm appointment details

**Closing:**
"Great, [Customer Name], we have you scheduled for [Date] between [Time Window]. You'll receive a confirmation email/text. Is there anything else I can help you with today? Thank you for choosing [Company]!"

### Booking Appointments

#### Creating a Job and Booking

**Step-by-Step:**

1. **Find or Create Customer**
   - **Search existing:** Type name, phone, or address in search bar
   - **Create new:** Click "New Customer" if first-time caller

2. **Verify/Enter Customer Information**
   - **Name:** First and last name
   - **Phone:** Primary contact number
   - **Email:** For notifications and invoicing
   - **Service Address:** Where work will be performed
   - **Billing Address:** If different from service

3. **Create Job**
   - Click **"Book Job"** or **"New Job"**
   - Select **Customer** and **Location**
   - Choose **Business Unit** (HVAC, Plumbing, Electrical)
   - Select **Job Type** (Service Call, Installation, Maintenance, Emergency)

4. **Add Job Details**
   - **Summary:** Brief description (e.g., "AC not cooling")
   - **Customer Notes:** Any special instructions
   - **Requested Date/Time:** When customer wants service
   - **Priority:** Normal, High, Emergency

5. **Coordinate with Dispatch** (or book directly if you have permissions)
   - Check technician availability
   - Assign to technician (or leave for dispatcher)
   - Confirm time window (e.g., 9am-11am)

6. **Confirm with Customer**
   - Read back appointment details
   - Provide time window
   - Explain what to expect
   - Give customer tech contact info (if available)
   - Offer to send confirmation

7. **Save Job**
   - Job created in ServiceTitan
   - Dispatch notified
   - Customer receives confirmation (if automated)

### Handling Different Call Types

#### Emergency Calls

**Identifying Emergencies:**
- No heat in winter / No AC in extreme heat
- Major water leak or flooding
- Gas leak
- Electrical hazard
- Complete system failure

**Procedures:**
1. **Stay Calm:** Reassure customer
2. **Gather Critical Info:** Name, address, phone, nature of emergency
3. **Safety First:** If dangerous (gas, electrical), advise customer to evacuate and call 911 if needed
4. **Immediate Response:** Alert dispatch or manager immediately
5. **Expedite Booking:** Priority scheduling, same-day or ASAP
6. **Follow Up:** Confirm tech en route, call customer with ETA

#### Service Requests

**Standard Workflow:**
- Identify problem
- Check service history
- Book appointment for earliest convenient time
- Set customer expectations

#### Estimate Requests

**Process:**
- Gather details about work desired
- Schedule assessment visit if needed
- OR if work is standard, create estimate remotely and send
- Explain estimate process and timeline
- Follow up after estimate presented

#### Follow-Up Calls

**Purposes:**
- Confirm appointments
- Check satisfaction after service
- Follow up on pending estimates
- Request reviews or referrals

**Tips:**
- Be friendly and professional
- Reference specific job or interaction
- Ask open-ended questions
- Note feedback in ServiceTitan

## Customer & Location Records

### Creating Customer Records

#### New Customer Setup

1. **Navigate to Customers**
   - Click **"Customers"** in left sidebar
   - Click **"New Customer"** or press **"+"**

2. **Enter Customer Information**
   - **Name:** First and last name
   - **Company Name:** If commercial customer
   - **Phone Numbers:** Primary, secondary, mobile
   - **Email:** For communications
   - **Customer Type:** Residential or Commercial

3. **Add Service Location**
   - **Address:** Street, city, state, ZIP
   - **Location Type:** Home, business, rental property
   - **Access Notes:** Gate codes, parking, pet information

4. **Billing Information** (if different from service location)
   - Billing address
   - Billing contact

5. **Communication Preferences**
   - Preferred contact method (phone, email, text)
   - Best time to contact
   - Opt-in/out for marketing

6. **Save Customer Record**

### Managing Customer Records

#### Updating Information

**When to Update:**
- Customer moves
- Phone number changes
- Email changes
- Billing information updates
- New equipment installed

**How to Update:**
1. Search and open customer record
2. Click **"Edit"**
3. Modify information
4. **Save**

#### Adding Notes

**Customer Notes:**
- Visible to all team members
- Include important information:
  - Special requests
  - Access instructions
  - Pet warnings
  - Payment preferences

**Adding Notes:**
1. Open customer record
2. Scroll to **"Notes"** section
3. Click **"Add Note"**
4. Type note
5. **Save**

### Location Records

**Multiple Locations:**
- Customers can have multiple service locations
- Common for landlords, property managers, commercial clients

**Adding Additional Locations:**
1. Open customer record
2. Go to **"Locations"** tab
3. Click **"Add Location"**
4. Enter address and details
5. **Save**

**Location-Specific Information:**
- Installed equipment at each location
- Access instructions per location
- Service history per location

## Invoicing & Payments

### Invoice Overview

**What is an Invoice?**
A bill for services performed, sent to customer for payment.

**Invoice Components:**
- Customer and location information
- Services performed
- Materials used
- Labor charges
- Taxes
- Discounts
- Total amount due
- Payment terms

### Creating Invoices (Office)

**When Office Staff Creates Invoices:**
- Technician completed work but didn't invoice
- Office handles billing for certain jobs
- Batch invoicing for multiple jobs

**Step-by-Step:**

1. **Open Job**
   - Find completed job
   - Go to **"Invoice"** tab

2. **Verify Services & Materials**
   - All work performed is listed
   - All materials used are included
   - Review quantities and pricing

3. **Add Missing Items**
   - Search pricebook
   - Add any missed services or materials

4. **Apply Discounts** (if applicable)
   - Membership discounts
   - Promotional offers
   - Manager-approved adjustments

5. **Review Total**
   - Subtotal
   - Taxes
   - Discounts
   - **Total Amount Due**

6. **Finalize Invoice**
   - Click **"Finalize"** or **"Complete"**
   - Invoice ready for customer

7. **Send Invoice**
   - **Email:** Send PDF via email
   - **Print:** Print and mail
   - **Text:** Send link via SMS (if configured)

### Processing Payments

#### Payment Methods

**Credit/Debit Card:**
1. Open invoice
2. Click **"Collect Payment"**
3. Enter amount
4. Select **"Credit Card"**
5. **Option A:** Enter card details manually
6. **Option B:** Use card reader/terminal
7. Process payment
8. Provide receipt

**Check:**
1. Collect physical check from customer
2. Open invoice
3. Click **"Collect Payment"**
4. Enter amount
5. Select **"Check"**
6. Enter check number
7. Take photo of check (if required)
8. **Save**

**Cash:**
1. Collect cash from customer
2. Open invoice
3. Click **"Collect Payment"**
4. Enter amount
5. Select **"Cash"**
6. **Save**
7. Provide receipt
8. Place cash in register/safe per company policy

**ACH/Bank Transfer:**
1. Open invoice
2. Click **"Collect Payment"**
3. Select **"ACH"**
4. Enter bank account info (or use saved)
5. Process transfer
6. Confirm transaction

**Partial Payments:**
- Customer pays portion of total
- Record partial payment
- Remaining balance due noted
- Follow up for remaining amount

#### Payment Tracking

**Outstanding Invoices:**
- Monitor unpaid invoices
- Send payment reminders
- Follow up with customers
- Escalate if needed

**Viewing Outstanding AR:**
1. **Reports > Financial > Outstanding AR**
2. See all unpaid invoices
3. Sort by age (30, 60, 90+ days)
4. Contact customers for payment

**Payment Plans:**
- For large invoices
- Work with customer on payment schedule
- Record payments as received
- Track remaining balance

## Accounting Workflows

### Overview of Accounting in ServiceTitan

**Key Activities:**
- Batch and export transactions
- Post invoices to accounting software
- Reconcile payments
- Process payroll data
- Manage QuickBooks integration

### Batching & Posting

**What is Batching?**
Grouping transactions (invoices, payments) before exporting to accounting software.

**Why Batch?**
- Organize transactions by period
- Easier reconciliation
- Clean export to QuickBooks/accounting system

**Batching Workflow:**

1. **Navigate to Accounting**
   - Click **"Accounting"** in sidebar
   - Select **"Batching"**

2. **Create Batch**
   - Click **"New Batch"**
   - Select date range (e.g., yesterday's transactions)
   - Review transactions included

3. **Review Batch**
   - Verify all invoices are complete
   - Ensure all payments recorded
   - Check for errors or missing data

4. **Post Batch**
   - Click **"Post"**
   - Batch locked (no further edits)
   - Ready for export

5. **Export to Accounting Software**
   - Click **"Export"**
   - Select format (QuickBooks, etc.)
   - Download file or sync automatically
   - Import into accounting system

### QuickBooks Integration

**Integration Benefits:**
- Automatic sync of invoices and payments
- Reduces duplicate data entry
- Keeps accounting up-to-date
- Minimizes errors

**How It Works:**
- ServiceTitan and QuickBooks connect via integration
- Transactions automatically transfer
- Chart of accounts mapped between systems
- Reconciliation simplified

**Managing Integration:**
1. **Settings > Accounting Integrations > QuickBooks**
2. **Connect Account** (if not yet connected)
3. **Map Accounts:** Link ServiceTitan categories to QuickBooks accounts
4. **Set Sync Frequency:** Real-time, daily, manual
5. **Monitor Sync Status:** Check for errors or issues

**Troubleshooting Integration:**
- Verify connection active
- Check for sync errors
- Re-map accounts if needed
- Contact support for persistent issues

### Accepting Payments

**Payment Processing Basics:**
- ServiceTitan integrates with payment processors
- Accept credit cards, debit cards, ACH
- Fees apply per transaction
- Funds deposited to business bank account

**Payment Collection Best Practices:**
- Collect payment at time of service when possible
- Offer multiple payment methods
- Make payment easy for customers
- Follow up on overdue invoices promptly

### Tax Exempt Customers

**Managing Tax Exemption:**
- Some customers are tax-exempt (non-profits, government, resellers)
- Requires valid exemption certificate on file

**Setting Up Tax Exemption:**
1. **Open Customer Record**
2. **Go to "Billing" or "Tax" section**
3. **Mark "Tax Exempt"**
4. **Upload Certificate** (PDF of exemption document)
5. **Save**

**Result:** Future invoices for that customer will not include sales tax

### Performance Pay Overview

**What is Performance Pay?**
Commission and bonus calculation for technicians based on sales and performance.

**Typical Commission Structures:**
- Percentage of revenue sold
- Flat rate per job
- Tiered bonuses based on thresholds
- Spiffs for specific products/services

**Office Staff Role:**
- Record technician splits and spiffs
- Verify commission data
- Export payroll reports
- Coordinate with payroll processing

**Integration Benefits:**

## Task Management

### Using Tasks in ServiceTitan

**What are Tasks?**
To-do items assigned to users, trackable in ServiceTitan.

**Common Task Types:**
- Follow up with customer
- Send estimate
- Verify payment received
- Complete paperwork
- Call customer for review

### Creating Tasks

**How to Create Task:**

1. **Navigate to Tasks**
   - Click **"Tasks"** in sidebar
   - OR create from customer/job record

2. **Create New Task**
   - Click **"New Task"** or **"+"**

3. **Enter Task Details**
   - **Title:** Brief description
   - **Assigned To:** Select user
   - **Due Date:** When it needs completion
   - **Priority:** Low, Normal, High
   - **Related To:** Link to customer, job, or estimate
   - **Notes:** Additional details

4. **Save Task**

### Managing Tasks

**Viewing Your Tasks:**
- **Tasks Dashboard:** See all your assigned tasks
- **Filter by:** Due date, priority, status
- **Sort by:** Due date, priority

**Completing Tasks:**
1. Open task
2. Complete the work
3. Mark as **"Complete"**
4. Add completion notes if needed

**Task Reminders:**
- Email or app notifications
- Reminders before due date
- Overdue task alerts

### Task Management Best Practices

**Do's:**
- ✅ Check tasks daily
- ✅ Prioritize urgent tasks
- ✅ Complete tasks promptly
- ✅ Mark complete when finished
- ✅ Communicate if unable to complete

**Don'ts:**
- ❌ Ignore tasks
- ❌ Let tasks go overdue without communication
- ❌ Forget to mark complete
- ❌ Create duplicate tasks

## Best Practices

### Customer Service Excellence

**Do's:**
- ✅ **Answer promptly:** Don't keep customers waiting
- ✅ **Be friendly:** Warm greeting and tone
- ✅ **Listen actively:** Understand customer needs
- ✅ **Be accurate:** Double-check details
- ✅ **Set expectations:** Clear about timing, process, pricing
- ✅ **Follow up:** Keep customers informed
- ✅ **Go extra mile:** Exceed expectations when possible

**Don'ts:**
- ❌ **Rush customers:** Take time to help properly
- ❌ **Be dismissive:** Every concern is valid
- ❌ **Overpromise:** Only commit to what you can deliver
- ❌ **Forget follow-up:** Close the loop
- ❌ **Argue:** Stay calm and professional
- ❌ **Pass the buck:** Own the issue and help resolve

### Data Entry Accuracy

**Importance:**
- Accurate data = better service
- Errors cause confusion and delays
- Financial accuracy critical

**Best Practices:**
- **Verify spelling:** Customer names, addresses
- **Confirm phone numbers:** Read back to customer
- **Double-check addresses:** Critical for technician routing
- **Complete all fields:** Don't leave blanks
- **Review before saving:** Catch mistakes early

### Time Management

**Prioritization:**
- **Urgent & Important:** Emergency calls, critical issues
- **Important but Not Urgent:** Follow-ups, scheduling
- **Urgent but Not Important:** Interruptions, some calls
- **Neither:** Minimize time on these

**Efficiency Tips:**
- Batch similar tasks (e.g., process all payments at once)
- Use templates for common communications
- Keyboard shortcuts in ServiceTitan
- Minimize distractions during focused work
- Take breaks to maintain energy

## Troubleshooting

### Common Office Staff Issues

**Can't Find Customer:**

**Symptoms:** Search returns no results

**Solutions:**
- Try alternative search terms (partial name, phone)
- Check spelling
- Search by phone number instead of name
- Look for variations (Bob vs. Robert, etc.)
- If truly new customer, create new record

**Payment Won't Process:**

**Symptoms:** Credit card declined, error message

**Solutions:**
- Verify card info entered correctly
- Check card reader connection
- Try different payment method
- Contact payment processor support
- Document issue and follow up

**Invoice Errors:**

**Symptoms:** Invoice total wrong, items missing

**Solutions:**
- Review services and materials listed
- Check for pricing errors
- Verify taxes calculated correctly
- Ensure discounts applied properly
- Edit invoice and correct before finalizing

**Phone System Issues:**

**Symptoms:** Calls not ringing, can't dial out

**Solutions:**
- Check phone system connection
- Verify phone powered on and plugged in
- Test with another phone
- Contact IT or phone system support
- Use backup phone line if available

**ServiceTitan Slow or Not Loading:**

**Symptoms:** System lag, pages won't load

**Solutions:**
- Refresh browser (F5 or Cmd+R)
- Clear browser cache and cookies
- Try different browser
- Check internet connection
- Restart computer
- Contact ServiceTitan support if widespread

### Common Questions

**Q: What if customer wants same-day service but schedule is full?**
A: Check for emergency slots or technician gaps. Offer first available (tomorrow). Add to wait list if someone cancels. Offer to call if opening arises.

**Q: How do I handle an angry customer?**
A: Stay calm, listen actively, empathize, apologize for their frustration (not necessarily admitting fault), offer solutions, escalate to manager if needed.

**Q: Customer says they already paid but invoice shows unpaid. What do I do?**
A: Check payment history in ServiceTitan. Look for payment on related jobs. Ask customer for payment method/date. If truly paid and not recorded, research and correct. If not paid, kindly explain and request payment.

**Q: What if I make a mistake entering data?**
A: Correct it immediately if caught. If already processed, notify supervisor and follow company procedure for corrections. Learn from it to avoid repeating.

**Q: Customer wants a discount. Can I approve it?**
A: Follow company policy. Some companies allow CSRs to give small discounts, others require manager approval. Know your limits and escalate if needed.
